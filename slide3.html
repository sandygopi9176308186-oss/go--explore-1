About Hyderabad

Hyderabad, the capital of Telangana, is a city that blends rich history, royal heritage, and modern technology. Known for its grand palaces, delicious biryani, and thriving IT industry, Hyderabad is one of India's most exciting destinations..

Hyderabad, the City of Nizams, is famous for its historical forts, palaces, lakes, and modern attractions. Whether you love history, food, or adventure, Hyderabad has something for everyone!

Why Visit Hyderabad?

Charminar – The Icon of Hyderabad 🕌 Built in 1591 by Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah. Famous for its four grand minarets & surrounding Laad Bazaar. A must-visit for history lovers & shopping enthusiasts! 📍Location: Old City

Golconda Fort – The Grand Fort of Hyderabad 🏰 Famous for its whispering walls & echoing secrets. Offers a stunning view of Hyderabad from the top. Enjoy the light & sound show in the evening. 📍Location: 11 km from Hyderabad

Hussain Sagar Lake – A Serene Escape ⛵ A man-made lake with a giant Buddha statue in the center. Enjoy boating, sunset views & night illumination. 📍Location: Between Hyderabad & Secunderabad
