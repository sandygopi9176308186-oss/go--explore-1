About Goa

Goa, India's beach paradise, is known for its stunning coastline, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese heritage, and adventurous water sports. Whether you want to relax on sandy beaches, explore historic forts, or party all night, Goa has something for everyone!.

Goa is India's smallest state, located on the western coast, along the Arabian Sea. It shares its borders with Maharashtra (north) and Karnataka (south and east).

Why Visit Goa?

Calangute Beach (Queen of Beaches) - The largest and most visited beach in Goa. Best for shopping, sunbathing, and water sports. 📍 Location: North Goa

Dudhsagar Waterfalls (Nature’s Wonder) - One of India’s tallest waterfalls (1020 feet). Best time to visit: Monsoon season (June - September). 📍 Location: Goa-Karnataka border

Aguada Fort (Portuguese Marvel) - A 17th-century fort built to guard against Dutch and Marathas. Offers breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea. 📍 Location: Near Candolim Beach
