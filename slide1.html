About agra

Agra, the city of the majestic Taj Mahal, is a historical gem in India’s cultural landscape. Situated on the banks of the Yamuna River in Uttar Pradesh, Agra was once the capital of the Mughal Empire, and its architectural wonders still reflect the grandeur of that era.

Agra, famous for its Mughal-era architecture, is home to some of India's most iconic landmarks. Here are the must-visit places in Agra:

Why Visit Agra?

Agra Fort - A UNESCO World Heritage Site, showcasing Mughal-era military architecture.

Fatehpur Sikri - A historic city with stunning palaces, courtyards, and the famous Buland Darwaza.

Jama Masjid - One of the largest mosques in India, known for its intricate Mughal design.
